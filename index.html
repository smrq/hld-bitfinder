Hyper Light Drifter Bitfinder — by smrq

Find those missing gearbits and keys!

Paste the contents of your save file here.

You can find your save files in the directory: C:\Users\<user>\AppData\Local\HyperLightDrifter.

The contents should be a long string of letters, numbers, plus signs, possibly with equals signs at the end. (This is called base64 encoding.)
